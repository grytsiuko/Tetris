
For each landed figure you earn 10 points.

If you collect the whole row, you earn bonus points:
	for 1 row  - 300
	for 2 rows - 1000
	for 3 rows - 2000
	for 4 rows - 4000

If collected row contains at least one square with a star, 
you earn 100 points more for each of them


1. Multi player right screen and Single player controls:

	- left/right arrows - move left/right
	- up arrow - rotate figure
	- down arrow - hold to speed up
	- space - pause


2. Multi player left screen controls:

	- A/D - move left/right
	- W- rotate figure
	- S - hold to speed up
	- Esc - pause
